Common questions

Can one person handle my project?

Yes. Most documentation projects are bottlenecked by communication, not headcount. You get a single point of contact who understands your product. For larger engagements, I bring in trusted specialists.

What does a typical project look like?

Fixed-price, 4–12 weeks depending on volume and complexity. Full ownership of all deliverables at handoff, plus a training session so your team can maintain the content going forward.

How does pricing work?

Every project starts with a free scoping call. I then deliver a fixed-price proposal with defined deliverables and timeline. No hourly billing. Most projects range from $8,000 to $25,000.

What tools do you use?

Docusaurus, Markdown/MDX, Git, VS Code, Figma for diagrams, and whatever fits your existing stack. If you don't have a docs platform yet, I'll recommend one and set it up as part of the project.
